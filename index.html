My name is Eduardo Castro, and I am currently doing my Ph.D. at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto, in Electrotechnical and Computers Engineering. I do my research work at INESC TEC in the Visual Computing and Machine Intelligence Group.

My research topic is the application of Computer Vision and Machine Learning techniques to Breast Cancer Diagnosis in Mammography. The ultimate goal is to contribute to attenuate the burden of this disease by developing accurate CAD systems usable in screening. I am currently interested in methods to make the optimization of Deep Learning methods more robust in scenarios of scarce and challenging data, such as those found in medical imaging. I am also interested in ways of integrating information from many regions of multiple images to make an accurate global diagnosis. I am generally curious, eager about learning and captivated by thought-provoking arguments.
